# Vueling Inflight Experience — UI Experience

> POC screens captured from the React frontend (`inflight-ui-poc`).
> Flight VY71299 BCN → FCO (Rome), Spanish language.

---

## Passenger Flow

The experience follows a natural passenger journey, from welcome to feedback.

---

### 1. Home — Welcome & Advisories

The passenger is greeted by name, sees their flight summary, and important advisories about airplane mode and mobile data.

<p align="center">
  <img src="screenshots/01-home.png" width="280" />
</p>

**Key elements:**
- Personalized greeting: "Bienvenido Ángel"
- Flight card: VY71299, BCN → FCO, 1h 45m, arrival 16:15
- Safety advisories: Airplane Mode, Mobile Data Off, Bluetooth OK, Seatbelt
- Navigation menu to all sections

---

### 2. Flight Details — Info, Baggage, Connections, Crew & Aircraft

Full flight information page: boarding pass data, luggage carousel, connecting flights table with gate and terminal info, crew details, flight map, and aircraft details.

<p align="center">
  <img src="screenshots/02-flight-details.png" width="280" />
</p>

**Key elements:**
- Flight info: BCN → FCO, Gate B23, Seat 15A
- Baggage claim: Carousel 5
- **Connecting flights table**: gate, terminal, same-terminal badge, status
- Crew: Captain, First Officer, 3 cabin crew
- Flight map with route visualization
- Aircraft: Airbus A320-200, registration EC-MXY

---

### 3. Recommendations — Weather, News, Highlights, Restaurants

AI-generated destination content: 3-day weather forecast, curated local news, top highlights and restaurants with emergency contacts.

## Screenshot Sequence (Demo Flow)

For a live demo, present the screens in this order:

| Step | Screen | Story |
|------|--------|-------|
| 1 | **Home** | "The passenger boards, opens the app, and immediately sees their flight + safety advisories" |
| 2 | **Flight Details** | "They check their gate, baggage carousel, and connecting flights — all before landing" |
| 3 | **Recommendations** | "AI-generated: weather, news, what to see, where to eat — in their language" |
| 4 | **Products** | "They order food and coffee from their seat" |
| 5 | **Entertainment** | "Destination-inspired music for the flight" |
| 6 | **Transport** | "How to get from the airport to the city — prices, times, tips" |
| 7 | **Feedback** | "Quick NPS survey before they deplane" |
